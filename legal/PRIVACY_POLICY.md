# Privacy Policy for Kinderwell

**Last Updated:** January 26, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Kinderwell. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
When you sign up for Kinderwell, we collect:
- **Email address** (from Google or Apple Sign-In)
- **Name** (from Google or Apple Sign-In)
- **User ID** (generated by our authentication provider)

### 2. Usage Data
We collect information about how you use the app:
- **Lesson progress** (which lessons you've completed)
- **Quiz results** (your answers and scores)
- **App usage timestamps** (when you access lessons)
- **Device information** (device type, OS version, app version)

### 3. Subscription Data
We collect information related to your subscription:
- **Subscription status** (active, expired, cancelled)
- **Purchase history** (subscription start date, renewal dates)
- **Payment information** is handled entirely by Apple and is NOT accessible to us

### 4. Automatically Collected Data
- **Device identifiers** (for app functionality)
- **IP address** (for security and service delivery)
- **Crash logs and diagnostics** (to improve app stability)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide the Service**
   - Authenticate your account
   - Save and sync your lesson progress across devices
   - Deliver personalized parenting content

2. **Improve the App**
   - Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
   - Fix bugs and improve performance
   - Develop new content based on user needs

3. **Communicate with You**
   - Send important service updates
   - Respond to support requests
   - Notify you about subscription changes

4. **Legal Compliance**
   - Comply with legal obligations
   - Enforce our Terms of Service
   - Protect against fraud and abuse

## Data Storage and Security

### Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely using:
- **Supabase** (cloud database provider, hosted on AWS)
- **Data centers located in:** United States
- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication protocols (OAuth 2.0)
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and monitoring
- Automatic logout after extended inactivity

### Data Retention

- **Active accounts:** We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Deleted accounts:** All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Legal requirements:** We may retain certain data if required by law

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### 1. Authentication Providers
- **Google Sign-In** ([Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Apple Sign-In** ([Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/))

### 2. Database and Backend
- **Supabase** ([Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy))
  - Stores user accounts and lesson progress
  - Hosted on secure AWS infrastructure

### 3. Payment Processing
- **Apple In-App Purchase** ([Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/))
  - We do NOT see or store your payment information
  - All transactions are processed by Apple

### 4. Subscription Management
- **Superwall** ([Superwall Privacy Policy](https://superwall.com/privacy))
  - Manages subscription status and paywall display
  - Does not access personal information

### 5. Analytics (Future)
- We may add analytics tools in the future to improve the app
- You will be notified if this changes

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### 1. Access Your Data
You can view your account information in the Settings screen of the app.

### 2. Correct Your Data
Contact us at kinderwellteam@gmail.com to update any incorrect information.

### 3. Delete Your Data
You can delete your account at any time:
- Go to **Settings** → **Delete Account**
- All your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
- This action cannot be undone

### 4. Export Your Data
Contact us at kinderwellteam@gmail.com to request a copy of your data.

### 5. Opt-Out of Communications
You can opt-out of non-essential communications through your device settings.

### 6. Withdraw Consent
You can withdraw consent for data processing by deleting your account.

## Children's Privacy

Kinderwell is designed for parents and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

The app collects information about your children (age, gender, name) but this information:
- Is stored locally on your device only
- Is never transmitted to our servers
- Is used solely for personalization within the app
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable laws.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with GDPR requirements.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
