Kinderwell Color Palette

Principle: Calm first. Encouragement second. Never noisy.

1. Primary Brand Color (Anchor)
Soft Sage Teal — “Calm Growth”

This will be the default color of the logo and app icon.

Why this works

Signals trust + care

Gender-neutral

Doesn’t feel “kidsy”

Stands out in the App Store without shouting

Hex (starting point):

#4F8F8B


Use it for:

App icon background

Primary buttons

Active states

Logo (primary version)

2. Background Color (Comfort Layer)
Warm Cream — “Safe Space”

Hex:

#FAF7F2


Why not pure white:

White feels clinical

Cream feels human

Use it for:

App backgrounds

Cards

Onboarding screens

3. Text Color (Authority without harshness)
Deep Charcoal — “Quiet Confidence”

Hex:

#2E2E2E


Use it for:

Headlines

Primary text

Icons (secondary)

Avoid pure black unless necessary.

4. Positive Feedback / Success Color
Soft Sun Peach — “Encouragement”

Hex:

#F4C7A1


Use sparingly for:

Completion states

Celebrations

Gentle highlights

Think “you did well”, not fireworks.

5. Secondary Accent (Optional, very light use)
Muted Sky Blue — “Clarity”

Hex:

#BFD8E2


Use for:

Info banners

Secondary CTAs

Progress indicators

6. Error / Warning (Non-alarming)
Soft Terracotta

Hex:

#D08C7A


This avoids aggressive red while still being clear.
