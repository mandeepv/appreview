What should we do when our partner had a bad day
First we want to have a radar for when our partner is grumpy
Particularly when it isn't about us they might be overwhelmed stressed or frustrated
We want to be on the lookout and notice as soon as we can
Just noticing sends a very important sign that we care about them and that we watch out for them. We have their back
Next we want to show concern
We want them to know that when they are upset we notice and care
This tells them that they are important to us in an empathetic way. We ask questions to figure out what is going on
You might already think you do these first two steps, but research has shown that we mostly don't
Next try to put yourself in their shoes in the situation that caused them distress in this example
His idea was shot down at work. Imagine. You are sitting at his desk when you finally reveal the great idea
You have been thinking about all weekend, but a colleague quickly shoots the idea down and dismisses it. How does that make you feel embarrassed?
Disappointed frustrated take a few minutes to try to feel those feelings. It's ok if it takes several minutes
finally in a kind and compassionate way try to name at least three emotions that they might be having and why
you will also want to avoid the common traps like
Minimizing what they are feeling or problem solving but labeling and avoiding traps are mostly covered in other lessons
For now try to remember that your responsibility is to help your loved ones empty their sandbags of emotions
Remember to have a radar for unhappiness
Gently ask about the situation put yourself in their shoes
So you can feel their emotions with them then help them label their feelings
Once you become practiced at this you may be stunned by the results. You'll start to understand for yourself
Why this is the number one way science has shown you can improve relationships
